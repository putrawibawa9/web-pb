About Putra Bali English Course

Putra Bali English Course is a local learning center focused on practical English speaking, listening, and communication skills. We combine modern teaching techniques, contextual materials, and small-group practice so learners progress confidently.

Our Mission

To make English accessible, usable, and relevant to the community — whether for study, work, or travel. We emphasize speaking fluency, real-life conversation drills, and building learners' confidence.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced local instructors who understand your needs.
  • Small class sizes and practical conversation drills.
  • Flexible schedules — morning, afternoon, evening.
  • Progress tracking and simple, goal-oriented lesson plans.
